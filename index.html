Sig2RKiDB

A Comprehensive Database of Sigma-2 Receptor Ligand Binding Affinities

About Sig2RKiDB

Sig2RKiDB integrates curated data from ChEMBL, S2RSLDB (2017), and recent literature to provide the most comprehensive collection of Sigma-2 receptor ligand binding affinity (Ki) values. Our database features:

  • • Standardized Ki measurements from multiple sources
  • • Detailed compound annotations
  • • Regular updates with newly published data
